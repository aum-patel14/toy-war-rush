How to Play

1

Swipe Left & Right

Drag your finger to steer your toy army through the lane. Dodge obstacles!

2

Pass Magic Gates

Hit green + and × gates to grow. Avoid red − gates that shrink your army.

3

Smash the Fortress

Reach the end with a big enough army to crush the block castle and win!

Features

🪖

Army Builder

Collect units, pass +10 and ×2 gates, grow your army to hundreds of toy soldiers.

⚡

Toy Evolution

Soldiers evolve into Knights, Robots, Mechs, Titans, and Ultra Titans.

🏰

Fortress Battles

Every level ends in an epic showdown against a colorful toy fortress.

🌍

10 Toy Worlds
