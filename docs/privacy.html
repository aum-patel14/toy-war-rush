Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Toy War Rush ("we", "our", or "the game") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Browser Demo (preview)

The free browser demo stores game progress (high score, highest level, sound settings) locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you clear browser storage.

Mobile App (Android — coming soon)

When the Android app launches, we may collect:

  • Gameplay analytics — level starts, wins, fails (via Firebase Analytics, if enabled)
  • Advertising data — ad identifiers for rewarded and banner ads (via Google AdMob, if enabled)
  • Crash reports — anonymous crash logs to fix bugs

We do not sell personal information. Children under 13 should use the app with parental consent.

Data We Do Not Collect (browser demo)

  • No account registration required to play the demo
  • No location tracking in the browser demo
  • No third-party analytics in the current browser demo
